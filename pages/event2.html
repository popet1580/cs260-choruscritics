Such Rhythm, Such Beat

The harmony of words, beeping machines and tinkling metal

Timpo
Words cannot express the joy I felt while witnessing such a beautiful event. The soft, gentle beat brings such an appreciation of life and its many intricacies. The precision of the one wielding the instruments was impeccable; I couldn't in all my many years of musical experience replicate this divine experience. I also cannot overlook the surgeon's contentment with his performance: that, to me, made the biggest impact.
Prim
This was quite an interesting performance. The movement between musicians and players was quite intricate. It was clear that the conductor was really calling all the shots. I mean, most conductors do, but this one was quite dominating and quite literally calling out what he wanted the other musicians to do, what instruments they should play or provide and HE did most of the music making. How odd. What about just waving the conductor stick around? What about SILENTLY leading his crew? I guess he was taking things in a new direction, but it realy put himself in the spotlight. It was a fun an unique experience.
